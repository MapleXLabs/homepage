About

Hi, I'm Dustin. I founded MapleX to do the kind of engineering work I love most: small teams, hard problems, software that ships.

My background is unusual on purpose. I hold a PhD in engineering and spent years in medical engineering research — including the Liver4Life project, where my team kept human livers alive outside the body for a week. That work taught me how to operate at the edge of what's possible while staying obsessive about reliability.

Today I run MapleX as a technical leader and hands-on builder. I lead product teams, architect systems, and write code — with AI woven through the whole stack. The result is software that gets to market fast without cutting the corners that matter.
